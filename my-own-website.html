    Mercedes Benz SLS AMG for sale

    Description

    The Mercedes Benz SLS AMG is a 2 seated car, developed by the AMG division of Mercedes with the help of racing driver David Coulthard. The SLS AMG is the successor to the Mercedes Benz SLR McLaren and is the spiritual successor to the 300SL Gulwing.

    Performance

    The SLS AMG is capable of reaching 0-60mph in 3.8 seconds and has a top speed of 196mph. The sports car is also capable of achieving a quarter mile time of 11.7 seconds and reaching a speed of 125mph.

    The SLS AMG has standard brakes which are 390mm in length. The SLS AMG has the option to have carbon-ceramic brakes which are 402mm in length. These brakes provide an increased stopping power.

    The fuel consumption for the SLS AMG is approximately 22 mpg.

    Safety

    The SLS AMG is equipped with ABS and a 3-stage electronic stability program. This means that it is able to detect when there is a loss of traction. The gullwing doors of the car provide easy exit for the driver and the passenger during a serious accident.
